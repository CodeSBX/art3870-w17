Company History & Objective

Citizens Mortgage was founded in 2006 and Roman L Perez Realty was founded in 2012 both in Modesto, CA. With over a decade of experience in providing loans, service, and expertise to homebuyers, our companies uphold an enduring presence in the lending community and by strategically meeting the highs and lows of the real estate and financial markets, have remained steadfast in their mission to provide affordable home financing, unsurpassed service, and a dedicated workforce to meet the Central Valley/Bay Area customers' needs for years to come!
